Frequently
Asked Questions

Can the AI qualify buyer leads instantly?

Yes. The AI asks about budget, location preferences, timeline, and financing status to identify serious, qualified buyers.

Does it schedule property showings?

Absolutely. The system books showings based on your availability, sends confirmation texts, and provides property addresses to clients.

Can sellers request home valuations?

Yes. The AI gathers property details and schedules CMA consultations to discuss pricing and listing strategies.

What about open house RSVP management?

The system collects RSVPs for open houses, sends reminders with directions, and tracks attendance for follow-up.

Can it handle investor inquiries?

Yes. The AI pre-qualifies real estate investors by asking about investment goals, property types, and financing capacity.

Does it follow up with showing attendees?

The system automatically follows up after showings to gather feedback, answer questions, and schedule additional viewings.

Can it coordinate closing schedules?

Yes. The AI sends reminders about inspection deadlines, appraisal appointments, and closing dates to keep transactions on track.

How does it nurture long-term leads?

The system maintains contact with not-yet-ready prospects, sending market updates and new listings until they're ready to act.
