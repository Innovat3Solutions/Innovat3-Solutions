Frequently
Asked Questions

What types of custom apps can you build?

We build web apps, mobile apps (iOS/Android), internal tools, customer portals, booking systems, CRMs, and automation dashboards tailored to your specific business processes.

How long does custom app development take?

Project timelines vary by complexity. Simple tools take 4-6 weeks, while complex multi-platform apps take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during discovery.

Do you use templates or build from scratch?

We build custom solutions from scratch using modern frameworks. While we leverage proven code patterns for efficiency, every app is designed specifically for your unique requirements.

Can the app integrate with my existing software?

Yes. We integrate with most business software including CRMs, payment processors, accounting systems, calendars, and proprietary databases via APIs and custom connectors.

Will I own the code and intellectual property?

Absolutely. You own 100% of the code, designs, and IP. We provide full source code access and documentation so you're never locked into our services.

What happens after the app launches?

We provide training, documentation, and ongoing support. We also offer maintenance packages for updates, bug fixes, feature additions, and infrastructure management.

Can you scale the app as my business grows?

Yes. We build apps with scalability in mind, using cloud infrastructure that grows with your user base and transaction volume without performance degradation.

How much does custom app development cost?

Projects typically range from $15k for simple tools to $100k+ for complex enterprise systems. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements and goals.
