Frequently
Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a high-performance website?

Most websites take 3-4 weeks from kickoff to launch. Complex sites with custom features may take 6-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.

What makes your sites faster than others?

We code from scratch using lightweight frameworks, optimize all images, implement lazy loading, and use modern caching strategies. Most WordPress sites are bloated with plugins we avoid entirely.

Do you redesign existing websites?

Yes. We can rebuild your existing site from the ground up with better performance, modern design, and improved conversion optimization while preserving your SEO rankings.

Will my site be mobile-responsive?

Absolutely. Every site we build is mobile-first, meaning it looks and performs perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops. We test across all major devices and browsers.

How do you handle SEO?

We bake SEO into every site with proper Schema markup, optimized meta tags, fast load times, semantic HTML, and mobile responsiveness. These are the foundations that help you rank.

Can you integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. We integrate with most CRMs, booking systems, payment processors, email platforms, and analytics tools. If it has an API, we can connect it.

What is your web development process?

We start with discovery (goals, audience, content), then design mockups for approval, followed by development, testing, and launch. You're involved at every stage with regular updates.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes. We offer monthly maintenance packages that include updates, security monitoring, backups, performance optimization, and content changes to keep your site running smoothly.
