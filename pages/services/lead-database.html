Support

Frequently
Asked Questions

How does lead capture automation work?

Every call, form submission, chat, and inquiry is automatically captured, tagged, enriched with contact info, and routed to your CRM or database in real-time.

Can it capture leads from multiple sources?

Yes. We integrate website forms, phone calls, social media, chat widgets, email inquiries, and third-party lead sources into one unified database.

What information does it collect about each lead?

Contact details, lead source, service interest, urgency, budget, timeline, conversation history, and behavioral data (pages visited, time spent, content downloaded).

Does it qualify leads automatically?

Yes. The system scores leads based on criteria you define (budget, timeline, fit) and routes hot leads to sales immediately while nurturing cold leads automatically.

Can it prevent lead leakage and missed follow-ups?

Absolutely. Every lead triggers automatic workflows, ensuring no inquiry falls through cracks. The system tracks response times and sends escalation alerts for missed follow-ups.

How does it integrate with my existing CRM?

We connect via native integrations or custom APIs to Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, ServiceTitan, and most CRMs. Leads sync bidirectionally with full field mapping.

Can it track lead ROI and source performance?

Yes. The system tracks which lead sources, campaigns, and channels produce the most revenue, helping you invest marketing budget in what actually works.

What about lead nurturing and follow-up?

The platform automates multi-touch follow-up sequences via email, SMS, and voice calls, keeping leads engaged until they're ready to buy or disqualify themselves.
