Support

Frequently
Asked Questions

How does automated review collection work?

After service completion, the system automatically sends SMS or email requests to customers asking for reviews on Google, Yelp, Facebook, or your preferred platforms.

Can it filter negative reviews before they go public?

Yes. The system uses sentiment analysis to detect unhappy customers and routes them to private feedback forms instead of public review sites, giving you a chance to resolve issues privately.

Which review platforms does it support?

We support Google Business Profile, Yelp, Facebook, Trustpilot, industry-specific platforms (Zillow, Healthgrades, Avvo), and your own website review system.

How do you get customers to actually leave reviews?

We use multi-channel outreach (text + email), perfect timing (right after service), simple one-click links, and optional incentives to maximize response rates.

Can it respond to reviews automatically?

The AI can generate personalized response drafts for you to approve. For positive reviews, it can auto-respond with thank-you messages. Negative reviews always route to you first.

Does it violate platform policies?

Not at all. We follow all platform guidelines, never buy fake reviews, and ensure all review requests are sent only to real customers who received your service.

Can I customize the review request messages?

Yes. You control the message timing, tone, and content. We provide templates that convert well, but you can customize everything to match your brand voice.

How quickly will I see results?

Most businesses see a 300-500% increase in review volume within the first month. The compound effect of more reviews improves your rankings and attracts more customers over time.
