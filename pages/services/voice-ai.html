Frequently
Asked Questions

Does the AI sound robotic?

Not at all. We use advanced voice synthesis with natural pauses, conversational filler words, and human-like intonation. Most callers cannot distinguish our AI from a human receptionist.

Can it schedule appointments directly?

Yes. The AI connects to your calendar (Google Calendar, Calendly, ServiceTitan, etc.) to check real-time availability and book appointments instantly without human intervention.

What happens if the AI doesn't know the answer?

The AI uses intelligent handoffs. If a query is too complex or outside its training, it takes a detailed message, flags it as urgent, and alerts your team via SMS/email immediately.

Is it HIPAA compliant for healthcare businesses?

Yes, we have specialized infrastructure for healthcare clients that is fully HIPAA compliant, ensuring patient data is secure, encrypted, and handled according to federal regulations.

Can the AI handle multiple calls simultaneously?

Absolutely. Unlike human receptionists, the AI can handle unlimited concurrent calls, ensuring no customer ever gets a busy signal or goes to voicemail.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are live within 7-14 days. We handle the technical setup, train the AI on your business specifics, and integrate with your existing tools and calendar systems.

Can I customize the AI's personality and script?

Yes. We tailor the AI's voice, tone, greeting scripts, and responses to match your brand voice. You have full control over how the AI represents your business.

What if I need to make changes after launch?

You can request script updates, add new services, or modify responses anytime. We implement changes quickly, usually within 24-48 hours of your request.
