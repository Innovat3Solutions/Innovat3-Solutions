# Service Graphic Prompts

Use these prompts to generate graphics that visualize the specific services offered by Innovate Solutions.
**Target Output**: Isometrically styled or flat vector infographic illustrations, similar to the niche graphics but focused on the service functionality.
**Colors**: Innovate Solutions Brand (Lime Green #84CC16, Slate Grey #94A3B8, White Background).

## 1. Lead Capture
- **Concept**: Super Bowl Watch Party Funnel.
- **Visual**: A digital "funnel" or landing page displayed on a tablet/screen. The screen shows a "Super Bowl Watch Party" invitation with a "Reserve Seat" button.
- **Action**: People icons flowing into the top of the funnel. At the bottom, a database/list is filling up with names.
- **Key Element**: A "Super Bowl" theme (football icon) but professional.
- **Prompt**: "Isometric illustration of a digital marketing funnel for a Super Bowl watch party. A tablet screen displays an event invitation with a 'Reserve Seat' button. Leads (icons) enter the funnel and turn into data rows in a CRM database. Green and slate grey color palette. Clean, modern, tech style."

## 2. Data Intelligence
- **Concept**: Remarketing based on Seasonality (Holiday/Summer).
- **Visual**: A split screen or cycle.
    - **Side A (Holiday)**: A pressure washer icon clearing snow/grime with a "25% Off Christmas Special" tag.
    - **Side B (Summer)**: A pool deck being pressure washed with a sun icon and "Summer Ready" tag.
- **Action**: Data points connecting the "Client List" to these seasonal offers.
- **Prompt**: "Isometric illustration of data intelligence and remarketing. A central database connects to two seasonal scenarios: Winter (Christmas 25% off pressure washing) and Summer (Pool deck cleaning). Data lines flow from the center to these offers, signifying targeted marketing. Green/Slate brand colors."

## 3. Workflow Automation
- **Concept**: Automated Invoicing & Payments.
- **Visual**: A calendar view with "Invoice Sent" checkmarks on specific dates. A "Gear" icon (Automation) connecting a completed job icon to an outgoing email icon.
- **Result**: Money bags/coins arriving automatically into a "Business Wallet" without a hand touching it (No manual effort).
- **Prompt**: "Isometric illustration of workflow automation for invoicing. A calendar shows scheduled invoice sending. An automation gear connects a 'Job Done' checkmark to an 'Invoice Sent' email. Money automatically flows into a digital wallet. Clean, efficient, frictionless process. Green and slate grey colors."

## 4. Growth Consulting
- **Concept**: Strategic Consultation & Efficiency.
- **Visual**: A consultant (professional figure) sitting at a table with a business owner. They are looking at a holographic projection or map of the business "Systems/Strategy".
- **Action**: The consultant is pointing to an efficiency gain (upward arrow/chart). No new "machinery" or resources added, just better organization (puzzle pieces fitting together).
- **Prompt**: "Isometric illustration of growth consulting. A consultant and business owner sit at a table planning strategy. They look at a business system diagram where puzzle pieces are being organized efficiently. An upward trending revenue arrow indicates growth without adding new resources. Professional, modern office vibe. Green and slate colors."
