## Image Placement in Blog Posts

Each blog post contains exactly 2 images:

1. **Hero Image** (line ~113)
   - Appears at the top of the article
   - Format: `../images/{blog-type}/hero-{blog-type}-{category}.png`
   - Dimensions: 800px wide, 400px height
   - Style: object-fit: cover, rounded corners, shadow

2. **Dashboard Image** (line ~157-186)
   - Appears in the "Results" section
   - Format: `../images/{blog-type}/dashboard-{blog-type}-{category}.png`
   - Dimensions: 800px max-width, auto height
   - Style: rounded corners, shadow, 1px border
