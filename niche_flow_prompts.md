# Niche Graphics Prompts

Matches the visual style of existing HVAC/Pool/Landscaping graphics:
- **Style**: Modern, clean, isometric or flat diagrammatic illustration.
- **Colors**: Innovate Solutions Brand (Lime Green #84CC16, Slate Grey #94A3B8, White Background).
- **Format**: Visual Comparison (Problem Path vs. Solution Path). No text lists.

## 5. General Contractors
- **Pain Point**: Lead Rot (Speed is key).
- **Concept**: Split path. Top (Grey): Lead enters, hits a '5 Day' clock, turns red/fades. Bottom (Green): Lead enters, hits 'Lightning Bolt' (Instant Response), turns into 'Handshake'.

## 6. Window Installers
- **Pain Point**: Estimating Lag (Wasted drive time).
- **Concept**: Top (Grey): Van driving a long winding road to a house with a red 'X'. Bottom (Green): Digital Checklist icon filters lead -> Van drives straight to house with Dollar Sign/Check.

## 7. Plumbers

## 8. Painters
