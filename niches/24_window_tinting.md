# Business Niche: Window Tinting & Auto Detail
## Product Name: ShadeTech

## Innovat3 Solutions Strategy
This is an upsell business. The money isn't in the wash; it's in the Ceramic Coating. We automate the upsell via **AI** and build value with a **Warranty Wallet App**.

## Pain Points We Solve
1.  **Low Ticket Size**: Customers just booking the basic package.
2.  **No-Shows**: Forgetting the appointment.
3.  **Education**: Customers not knowing why "Ceramic" is worth $1,000.

## Our Service Implementations

### 1. Custom App Development
-   **Service**: **"Warranty Wallet" App**
-   **How it Works**: Digital warranty card for their tint/coating.
-   **Why It Matters**: Proof of value and keeps the brand in their pocket for the next car.

### 2. Workflow Automation
-   **Service**: **The Upsell Sequence**
-   **How it Works**: Customer books a tint. System emails: "Protect your paint while you're here? Add Ceramic for 20% off."
-   **Why It Matters**: Increases revenue per car without extra marketing cost.

### 3. Outreach Automation
-   **Service**: **Seasonal Protection**
-   **How it Works**: "Winter is coming. Protect your paint from salt."
-   **Why It Matters**: Drives demand during slower months.

### 4. Review Automation
-   **Service**: **Showroom Reviews**
-   **How it Works**: "Your car looks amazing. Share a pic!"
-   **Why It Matters**: High visual appeal drives social shares.

### 5. High-Converting Funnel
-   **Service**: **"Visualizer" Funnel**
-   **How it Works**: "See what your car looks like tinted."
-   **Why It Matters**: Engaging tool that captures the lead.

## ROI & Impact
-   **Revenue**: Automated upsells can add **$100-$300** per booking.
-   **Retention**: Reminder texts reduce no-shows by **40%**.
