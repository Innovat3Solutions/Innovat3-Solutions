# Niche Statistic Graphic Prompts

Use these prompts to generate "Informational Statistic" graphics.
**Target Output**: Isometrically styled or flat vector infographic illustrations.
**Key Focus**: Highlighting the *pain point statistic* to drive urgency.
**Colors**: Innovate Solutions Brand (Lime Green #84CC16, Slate Grey #94A3B8, White Background).

## Home & Property Services

### 1. Pool Cleaners & Maintenance
- **Stat**: "Home service businesses miss **27-30%** of inbound calls."
- **Visual Concept**: A split view. Left side: A smartphone ringing with a red "Missed Call" notification icon. Right side: A bucket with a hole, water (labeled "Revenue") leaking out. Large text "**30%**" in the center.

### 2. Pool Contractors & Construction
- **Stat**: "Responding within **5 minutes** increases conversion odds by **21x**."
- **Visual Concept**: A speedometer or stopwatch showing "5 Mins" in green. Next to it, a graph bar rocketing upwards labeled "21x Conversion". Background: A modern pool blueprint.

### 3. Landscaping & Lawn Care
- **Stat**: "Field service businesses lose up to **$126,000 annually** due to poor communication."
- **Visual Concept**: A pile of money (cash stacks) fading away or turning into leaves blowing in the wind. Large text "**$126k Lost**" in bold red.

### 4. HVAC Services
- **Stat**: "**62%** of calls go unanswered by SMBs during peak times."
- **Visual Concept**: A thermostat dial set to "62%". The AC unit is frozen or has a red warning light. Text "Unanswered Calls" acts as the temperature reading.

### 5. General Contractors
- **Stat**: "**78%** of business goes to the first responder."
- **Visual Concept**: A race track finish line. A green race car (Innovate Solutions user) crossing first, holding a trophy. Gray cars (competitors) are far behind. Large text "**78% Wins**".

### 6. Window Installers
- **Stat**: "**68%** of leads go cold if not contacted within the first hour."
- **Visual Concept**: A frosty window pane with "68%" written in the condensation/ice. A thermometer showing freezing temperature. Subtext: "Leads Go Cold".

### 7. Plumbers
- **Stat**: "**50-80%** of potential revenue is lost to competitors."
- **Visual Concept**: A pipe leaking gold coins instead of water. A wrench trying to tighten the bolt. Large text "**80% Loss**" near the leak.

### 8. Painters
- **Stat**: "No-show rates for home evaluations can hit **20-30%**."
- **Visual Concept**: A painter's palette where 30% of the paint colors are grayed out or missing. An empty easel with a "No Show" sign.

### 9. Electricians
- **Stat**: "Skilled trade businesses spend **40% of their day** on non-billable admin/travel."
- **Visual Concept**: A clock face where 40% of the slice is gray (labeled "Admin/Travel"). The rest is green ("Billable"). An electrician looking at a map, frustrated.

### 10. Property Management
- **Stat**: "Property managers spend **60% of their time** on communication."
- **Visual Concept**: A smartphone screen overflowing with notification bubbles (Email, SMS, Phone). A battery icon draining fast. Large text "**60% Drain**".
